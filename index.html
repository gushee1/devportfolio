What I do

During my 4 years studying Computer Science at Michigan, I've had many oppurtunities to learn and apply new skills in differeny ways. Here are the skills that I'm proudest of.

Game Development

I use Unity, Unreal, and bespoke game engines to design and build original games across many genres. I am comfortable in many facets of the game design pipeline, and have a solid foundation in game design principles such as composition, guidance, and others.

XR Development

I have used ARKit, AFCore, and other frameworks in both Unity and Unreal to create VR and AR applications. I have used Unreal to make game-like simulations for education applications. I have also used Unity to create mobile AR applications for iOS.

App Development

From industrial monitoring applications to desktop game engines to mobile apps, I have designed and buiilt applications of all varieties. Effective Communication
