Projects

  • Metroid (Unity Remaster)
  • Elemental Unity

For Metroid, I implemented Samus's logic with regard to jumping, running, sprite control, and animation. Additionally, I created all enemies and programmed their movement, as well as other associated logic with enemies (pickup drops, etc.)
For our custom level, I came up with the mechanic idea and planned and implemented the level design. Additionally, I wrote the code to toggle between metal and normal states.

Elemental Unity was a solo project that was completed by me over the span of two weeks. Most art and sound was was taken from royalty free websites licensed for educational use.
